**Command Name:**
`a!setbal`

**Bot Response:**
```{perset;{args;1}_Balance;{args;2}}{args;1}'s balance was set to: :economy_gem: `{args;2}` Gems.```

**Command Description:**
Sets a user's balance.

**Usage:**
`a!setbal <user> <amount>`

**Example:**
`a!setbal @JaMcSu 10`

**Recommended Settings:**
* Exempt Roles - Equivalent of: (Administrator or Moderator)
